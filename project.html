Holi is a Hindu festival that celebrates the arrival of spring, the triumph of good over evil, and the divine love of Radha and Krishna Holi originated in India and Nepal, and is now celebrated in other parts of Asia and the Western world Holi symbolizes the victory of good over evil, and the importance of forgiving others and making friends again. It also celebrates the blossoming of love and new life Family and friends gather to playfully throw colorful paint and bright powders at each other and into the air, fully immersing themselves from head to toe in color. Additionally, a troupe plays a traditional wooden drum called a dhol while dancing amid the colors. Each color carries a different meaning during Holi.
